What is needlepointing?

Needlepointing is a type of embroidery worked on an even-weave canvas. Normal embroidery differs from needlepointing in that it uses a soft cloth and requires an embroidery hoop. With needlepointing, One can stitch the stiff canvas free hand or find a canvas that has a design already painted on it. This type of canvas work began when ancient Egyptians used small slanted stitches to sew their canvas tents.
